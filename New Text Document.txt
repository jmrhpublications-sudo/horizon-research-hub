About the Journal
The Journal of Multidisciplinary Research Horizon (JMRH) is a peer-reviewed, open-access scholarly journal dedicated to the publication of original research across multidisciplinary domains. The journal provides a platform for researchers, academicians, and professionals to disseminate scholarly work.
All manuscripts undergo a rigorous double-blind peer review process to ensure quality, originality, and academic integrity.

ABOUT JOURNAL
The Journal of Multidisciplinary Research Horizon (JMRH) is an open-access academic journal committed to promoting interdisciplinary research and academic excellence. The journal publishes original research articles, review papers, and case studies.
The journal strictly follows ethical publishing practices in accordance with UGC regulations and international publication standards.

REVIEWER BOARD
JMRH follows a structured double-blind peer review system supported by qualified subject experts.
Reviewer Responsibilities
•	Maintain confidentiality of manuscripts 
•	Provide unbiased and constructive evaluation 
•	Adhere to ethical review standards 
•	Declare any conflict of interest 
________________________________________
AUTHOR GUIDELINES
Submission
Authors must submit original, unpublished manuscripts not under consideration elsewhere.
callforpapers@jmrh.in
________________________________________
Formatting
•	MS Word (.doc/.docx) 
•	Times New Roman, Size 12 
•	Double spacing 
•	APA 7th Edition 
________________________________________
Manuscript Structure
•	Title Page 
•	Abstract (150–250 words) 
•	Keywords 
•	Introduction 
•	Review of Literature 
•	Methodology 
•	Results and Discussion 
•	Conclusion 
•	References 
________________________________________
PUBLICATION ETHICS
•	Plagiarism must not exceed 10% 
•	Follow UGC and COPE guidelines 
•	No duplicate submission 
•	Ethical violations will result in rejection or retraction 
________________________________________
PEER REVIEW PROCESS
1.	Initial Screening (Scope + Plagiarism Check) 
2.	Double-Blind Peer Review (Minimum 2 Reviewers) 
3.	Editorial Decision 
Average Review Time: 3–4 weeks
________________________________________
APC (ARTICLE PROCESSING CHARGE)
The Journal of Multidisciplinary Research Horizon (JMRH) charges an APC of ₹650 (INR) per accepted manuscript.
Policy
•	Charged only after acceptance 
•	Does not influence editorial decision 
•	Covers: 
o	Peer review 
o	Copyediting 
o	Formatting 
o	Online publication and archiving
